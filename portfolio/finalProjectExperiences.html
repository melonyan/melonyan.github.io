My Projects

Sales Manual

This group project is a kitchen planning and retail sales manual to help new salespeople in IKEA learn how to present, guide the customer, and provide the service of kitchen planning. With emphasis on ensuring customer satisfaction and loyalty.

My part in this group project was to give an outline of our chosen company, in this case IKEA Holding B.V., their target audience, and our chosen product, kitchen planning and retail. My other tasks included visualizing the sales process and approaching phase. And finally, my final duty was to construct an elevator pitch for our company and product.

We had an amazing group dynamic, had common goals, and were all hard-working through which we were able to achieve the highest marks.
